		Heat
			\Delta H = q
				@ dP/dt=0
				q		(J)		Heat
				H		(J)		Enthalpy
				P		(Pa)	Pressure
				t		(s)		time
			Heat of Fusion:			\Delta H_{fus}	(J/mol OR J/g)
				@ dT/dt = 0
				Solid + \Delta H_{fus} --> Liquid
			Heat of Vaporization:	\Delta H_{vap}	(J/mol OR J/g)
				@ dT/dt = 0
				Liquid + \Delta H_{vap} --> Gas

Laws of Thermodynamics
	0th: 
		T_A = T_B \land T_B = T_C \implies T_A = T_C
		
	1st: 
		\Delta E = Q + W
			E		Internal Energy
			q		Heat
			w		Work
	2nd: 
		\Delta S \geq q/T
			S		(J/K)	Entropy
			q		(J)		Heat Energy
			T		(K)		Temperature 
		Reversible:		\Delta S = q/T
		Irreversible:	\Delta S > q/T
