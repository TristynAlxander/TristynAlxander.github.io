More Laws & Identities:
	Law Of Sines: $$ \frac{\sin( \alpha )}{a} = \frac{\sin( \beta )}{b} = \frac{\sin( \gamma )}{c} = 2R_{c} $$ $$ A = \frac{a \, b \, \sin{\gamma}}{2} $$ $$ A = \frac{a \, b \, c}{4 * r} $$
	Law Of Cosines: $$ a^2 = b^2+c^2-2 \, b \, c \,\cos ( \alpha ) $$
	Law Of Tangents: $$ \frac{a-b}{a+b} = \frac{\tan \left ( \frac{ \alpha - \beta }{2} \right )}{\tan \left ( \frac{ \alpha + \beta }{2} \right )} $$
	Mollweides Formulas: $$ \frac{a+b}{c} = \frac{\cos( \frac{ \alpha - \beta}{2} )}{\sin( \frac{\gamma}{2} )} $$ $$ \frac{a-b}{c} = \frac{\sin( \frac{ \alpha - \beta}{2} )}{\cos( \frac{\gamma}{2} )} $$
	Pythagorean Identities: $$ \sin^2( \theta ) + \cos^2( \theta ) = 1 $$ $$ \tan^2( \theta )+1 = \sec^2( \theta ) $$ $$ 1+\cot^2( \theta ) = \csc^2( \theta ) $$
