Quantitative: Numbers.
	Significance
		Significant: Sum of sufficiently defined units.
		Uncertain: Approximate addition partial unit.
		Spurious: All digits after the Uncertain Digit
		Addition & Subtraction: Uncertain Place Maintained
		Multiplication & Division: Significant Count Maintained
		Rounding: Cuts off Spurious Digits
	Scientific Notation
		Factor into s &times; 10^{x}
		First Factor -10 < s < 10
		Round in Base 10
	Particular Measurement Types
		Markings: Sum Significant Digits, Add Uncertain, Convert to Scientific, Round.
		Readings: Last Place Uncertain.
Qualitative: Variable Alternatives.
