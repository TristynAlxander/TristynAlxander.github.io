Growth
	Long Average Growth Rate (LAGR):	{Y_{t} - Y_{0}}{Y_{0} (t_{2} - t_{1})}
	Average Annual Growth Rate (AAGR):	\frac{\sum_{t_{0}}^{t_{n}} \frac{ Y_{t} - Y_{t-1} }{Y_{t-1}}}{t_{n} - t_{0}}
	Continuous Time Growth Rate (CTGR):	g = \frac{ln(Y_{t})-ln(Y_{0})}{t}
	Compound annual Growth Rate (CAGR):	G_{y} = e^{g} - 1

Capital Accumulation Equations
	Basic: 
		\dot{K} = s Y- \delta K
			s			Savings/Investment Rate
			Y			Output
			\delta		Depreciation
			K			Capital 
			
	Developed:
		\dot{\hat{k}} = s \hat{y} - \delta \hat{k} - n \hat{k} - g \hat{k}
			\dot{\hat{k}}		derivative wrt time of Capital per Effective Work
			s \hat{y}			Steady-State Savings/Investment per worker
				s				Savings/Investment Rate
				\hat{y}			Output per Effective Work 
			\delta \hat{k}		Capital Decay
				\delta			Depreciation
				\hat{k}			Capital per Effective Work
			n \hat{k}		Population Growth
				n				Rate of Labour Growth
				\hat{k}			Capital per Effective Work

				g				Rate of Technological Growth
