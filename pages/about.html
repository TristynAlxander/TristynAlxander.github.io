About Me: I consider myself a simple person who merely wants to survive comfortably. The issue is that I don't want to die. Sure, most people don't, but I'm not resigned to the inevitability of death. As such, I'm determined to solve aging and every other non-congenital, non-contagious disease. As such, my long-term goal is to create a cell-mediated gene-delivery system.
